Lasagne Recipie

Some tasty looking lasagne

It contains all sorts of nice stuff

  • Meat
  • Bolognse
  • Beschemel
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. cook the meat
  2. add the bolognse
  3. get the bowl ready
  4. layer the pasta and bolognse
  5. Add the beschemel on top
  6. Cover with cheese
  7. Bake for 45 minutes
